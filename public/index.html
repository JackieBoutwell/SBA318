Cooking Website
  • Search and Browse: Users can search for recipes using keywords, such as ingredients, cuisine type, cooking method, or dietary restrictions. They can also browse through categories like breakfast, dinner, desserts, vegetarian, vegan, etc.


  • User Interaction: Users can interact with recipes in several ways. When they sign in they can rate recipes, leave comments or reviews, and sometimes even upload their own photos of dishes they've made using the recipe.
